Frequently Asked Questions

What if we invest and it does not work?

We set measurable goals such as qualified leads and booked calls. Scope is aligned to those goals, copy is written for conversions, and results are tracked with analytics and pixels when ads are used. You can also review the Zero Risk, 100% Money Back policy on the guarantee page.

See the guarantee

How long does a typical project take?

Most websites take 2 to 4 weeks depending on pages and assets. Timeline depends on content readiness and feedback speed. You receive checkpoints for wireframe, copy, design, build, and launch.

What exactly is included?
  • Discovery and positioning for your offer and audience
  • Wireframe and information architecture
  • Conversion copy for key pages and calls to action
  • Design and build with responsive layout and accessibility basics
  • SEO basics with structure, titles, meta, and alt text
  • Performance pass and quality assurance
  • Launch support and 1 month of email support

What is not included by default?

Ongoing ad management, professional photography or video, complex back end apps, third party subscription fees, and advanced SEO retainers are not included by default. If you need any of these, they can be quoted separately.

Can you work on our existing site or do you rebuild?

I start with a quick audit. If the foundation is stable, I improve UX, copy, and performance. If the platform blocks results, I propose a lean rebuild that keeps your branding and content where possible.

What stack do you use and who owns the assets?

Clean HTML, CSS, and JS with a lightweight CMS when needed and integrations like MailerLite or HubSpot. You own the code, the domains, and the accounts. Everything is handed over with access and documentation.

How do we measure success after launch?

Practical and zero maintenance. We measure leads and booked calls.

  • Baseline. Current weekly average from the last 30 days.
  • Compare. Same numbers at day 30 and day 60 after launch.
  • Auto log. Forms send to your email and to a spreadsheet. Bookings from your scheduler are counted in the same sheet.
  • Scope. We measure interest created. Closing sales remains with your team.

What if leads rise but sales do not?

That points to qualification or the sales process, not traffic quality. The site and campaigns are accountable for awareness and interest. If you use the mini CRM page, you can tag outcomes to see patterns and adjust scripts or offers.

Can we have a simple place to track leads?

Yes, as an optional add on. I can add a light CRM page inside the site with a lead table, quick filters, and CSV export. Submissions and bookings appear automatically.

What about SEO?

SEO basics are included: technical structure, on page tags, speed, and accessibility. Content strategy, backlinks, and ongoing SEO campaigns can be quoted if needed.

What do you need from us to start?
  • Brand assets and guidelines if available
  • Your offer, services, and pricing
  • Three reference sites you like and why
  • Preferred tone of voice and no go areas
  • Access to current site, domain, and tools

How do payments work?

Simple structure: 50 percent to start and 50 percent before launch. You receive an invoice for each payment. Bank transfer is accepted. Other methods can be arranged if required.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes. If you want steady momentum, you can add the Monthly Growth Engine for updates, content, and small campaigns. If you prefer ad hoc help, you can request support when needed.

We do not have a marketing team or technical skills

I am your team. You share goals and assets, I handle planning, copy, design, build, and launch. After launch I take care of all edits and updates for you. If you want zero maintenance, the Monthly Growth Engine keeps improvements, content, and fixes off your plate.
