What results should we expect?

  • Marketing Plan: 3-month roadmap with actions you can execute.
  • Social Kit: consistent presence that builds trust and drives messages.
  • Growth Partner Program: steady monthly improvements and content.

See also the Zero Risk, 100% Money-Back Guarantee.

How long does a typical project take?

Websites: usually 2–4 weeks depending on pages and content readiness.

Marketing Plan: 7–10 working days.

Social Kit: 5–7 working days.

We agree milestones (copy → design → build → launch) and you get live previews in the browser.

What’s included and what isn’t by default?

Included items are listed in each service or add-on. By default, ongoing ad management, complex back-end apps, professional photo/video and advanced long-term SEO retainers are not included.

Need something extra? We can add it as an add-on or quote it separately.

Can you improve our current website or do you rebuild?

I start with a short audit. If the foundation is stable, I improve UX, copy and structure. If the platform blocks results, I recommend a lean rebuild that preserves your brand and content where possible.

What exactly is the Growth Partner Program?

It’s a monthly partnership that keeps your website and marketing moving. You get website updates, a strategy session each month, new content (social posts, emails, funnels), technical maintenance and priority support.

Ideal if you prefer one trusted person handling web + marketing instead of coordinating multiple agencies.

Do you offer professional sales training for our team?

Yes. I offer a complete online program called "Lead-to-Deal" Professional Sales System. A structured sales course that teaches your team how to turn new leads into real clients with confidence and consistency.

Who it’s for

Perfect for entrepreneurs, receptionists and small teams who handle leads but want a calm, professional structure to improve results without pressure. The training is entirely in English.

Outcome

After completing the "Lead-to-Deal" Professional Sales System, your team will have a clear, repeatable method to handle every lead from the first message to a confirmed sale with more confidence, clarity and higher conversion rates.

Result: A motivated team that feels competent, confident. Proud to represent your business and clients who say “yes” more often.

Do you work in English or Norwegian?

Both. I collaborate with a professional Norwegian translator, which allows me to deliver projects in perfect English and natural, native-level Norwegian.

Depending on your goals, we can:

  • 🗣️ Build your entire website or marketing materials fully in Norwegian for a local audience.
  • 🌍 Or create a bilingual/multilingual version (EN, NO, IT, FR, DE) so your business reaches both local and european/international clients.

This dual-language approach increases trust with customers and expands visibility abroad, giving your business a professional, global presence from day one.

How do quotes and payments work?

Every project starts with a free 45-minute consultation to map goals and scope. You’ll receive a tailored quote in NOK. A deposit reserves your slot; the balance follows agreed milestones or monthly retainers for ongoing work.

Invoices are issued in NOK. If you need split payments, we can set up a schedule aligned to the project phases.

Who owns the website and accounts after launch?

You do. Domains, hosting, email/marketing tools, and code are set up under your ownership. Access and documentation are handed over at the end.

Do we need to write the content?

I write or refine the core copy (headlines, calls to action, key sections) based on a short interview and your materials. If you have existing text, I adapt it into clear, conversion-focused messaging.

How do revisions work?

Each phase includes a review round (copy → design → build). Feedback is gathered at checkpoints to keep timelines on track. Additional rounds can be added if needed.

Can we pause or cancel the monthly program?

Yes. The Growth Partner Program is a monthly partnership with a short minimum term to start. After that, you can scale hours up/down, pause for a month, or cancel with notice — simple and flexible.

How do we communicate during the project?

We keep it simple: a shared task board for progress, email for updates, and short calls when decisions are needed. You’ll always know what’s done, what’s next, and what’s needed from you.
